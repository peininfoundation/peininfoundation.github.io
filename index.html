問題所在 · The Problem

被科技遺忘的一代

Chinese American seniors are one of the fastest-growing and most underserved populations in the United States. The technology gap they face is not a minor inconvenience — it is a health equity crisis.

600,000+ Chinese American seniors in the US with limited English proficiency
72% of Chinese immigrant seniors report feeling socially isolated
0 existing products combine Mandarin AI, US context, and emergency safety in one solution
3 hrs average delay when language barriers prevent seniors from calling for help
🌐

語言障礙 · Language Barrier

Siri, Alexa, Google — all English-first. For Mandarin-speaking seniors, every interaction with technology is a barrier. Asking for directions, reading a medication label, reaching emergency services — all become impossible without fluent English.

🏠

社會孤立 · Social Isolation

Language exclusion leads directly to social isolation, which the CDC links to a 50% increased risk of dementia and a 29% increased risk of heart disease. For Chinese American seniors, isolation is not just loneliness — it is a measurable health risk.

🚨

安全危機 · Safety Crisis

When emergencies happen — a fall, a stroke, a sudden illness — seniors who cannot communicate in English face life-threatening delays in care. Existing alert systems like Life Alert require the user to speak English to describe what happened.

📵

市場空白 · Market Gap

No competitor currently combines Chinese language support, US local context, senior-friendly design, and emergency safety features in a single product. This is not a crowded market — it is an underserved community waiting for a solution built for them.

解決方案 · Our Solution

陪您 — 以您的語言，守護您

PéiNín is the first Chinese-language AI companion app built specifically for Chinese American seniors in the United States. We combine conversational AI, emergency safety, and daily wellness — in one simple, senior-friendly interface that requires no English, no complex setup, and no prior technology experience.

語言陪伴 · LANGUAGE 🗣️

說您的語言 · Full Mandarin AI Companion

Seniors converse naturally in Mandarin — asking questions, getting reminders, receiving local information, and feeling genuinely heard. No English required. No translation needed. Just conversation, the way it should be.

緊急求助 · SAFETY 🚨

一鍵緊急求助 · One-Touch SOS Alert

A single button instantly notifies designated family members with the senior's location and situation — in both Chinese and English. Our system bridges the language gap that prevents seniors from reaching help when they need it most.

日常關懷 · WELLNESS 🌅

每日健康問候 · Daily Wellness Check-in

Every morning, PéiNín greets the senior and checks in. If there is no response within 30 minutes, family members are automatically notified — reducing false positives while ensuring no senior is left unaccounted for.

關於我們 · About Us

我們是誰

PéiNín Foundation was founded by Chinese Americans who lived this problem firsthand. We watched our own family members — grandparents, parents, aunts and uncles — struggle with isolation, language barriers, and the quiet fear of being unreachable in an emergency. We knew the technology to help them existed. We also knew no one had built it for them.

We are technologists, caregivers, and community advocates. We have built a working AI prototype that has already been tested with real seniors in the San Francisco Bay Area. We are not building this from a boardroom — we are building it from lived experience, user feedback, and deep respect for the community we serve.

我們的總部位於舊金山灣區，服務全美華裔社區。
Headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area, proudly serving the greater Chinese American community across the United States.
