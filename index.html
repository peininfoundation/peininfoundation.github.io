問題所在 · The Problem

被科技遺忘的一代

Chinese American seniors are one of the fastest-growing and most underserved populations in the United States. The technology gap they face is not a minor inconvenience — it is a health equity crisis.

750,000+ Chinese American seniors age 65+ in the US — a fast-growing, underserved population (US Census, 2023)
2 in 3 Chinese American seniors 65+ have limited English proficiency (AARP, 2023)
0 existing products combine Mandarin/Cantonese AI, US local context, and emergency safety in one solution
69% of Chinese adults age 55+ have limited English proficiency, creating life-threatening barriers in emergencies (US Census Bureau)
🌐

語言障礙 · Language Barrier

Siri, Alexa, Google — all English-first. For Mandarin and Cantonese-speaking seniors, every interaction with technology is a barrier. Asking for directions, reading a medication label, reaching emergency services — all become impossible without fluent English.

🏠

社會孤立 · Social Isolation

Language exclusion leads directly to social isolation. When seniors cannot communicate with neighbors, healthcare providers, or community services, they withdraw — losing the human connection that is essential to healthy aging. For Chinese American seniors, isolation is not just loneliness — it is a documented health and safety risk.

🚨

安全危機 · Safety Crisis

When emergencies happen — a fall, a stroke, a sudden illness — seniors who cannot communicate in English face life-threatening delays in care. Existing alert systems like Life Alert require the user to speak English to describe what happened.

📵

市場空白 · Market Gap

No competitor currently combines Chinese language support, US local context, senior-friendly design, and emergency safety features in a single product. This is not a crowded market — it is an underserved community waiting for a solution built for them.

解決方案 · Our Solution

陪您 — 以您的語言，守護您

PéiNín is the first Chinese-language AI companion app built specifically for Chinese American seniors in the United States. Six integrated features — in one simple, voice-first interface that requires no English, no complex setup, and no prior technology experience.

語言陪伴 · LANGUAGE 🗣️

中文語音對話 · Mandarin & Cantonese Voice Conversation

Seniors converse naturally in Mandarin or Cantonese — asking questions, getting help, sharing their day. No English required. No typing needed. Just voice conversation, in the language they call home.

緊急求助 · SAFETY 🚨

一鍵SOS緊急求助 · One-Touch SOS Emergency Alert

A single button instantly notifies designated family members — not 911 — with the senior's location and situation in both Chinese and English. Designed for seniors who cannot communicate emergencies in English.

智能提醒 · REMINDERS 💊

每日智能提醒 · Daily Smart Reminders

Personalized voice reminders for medications, hydration, and meals — set by family members through the dashboard. If a senior does not confirm, family is automatically notified. Safety without surveillance.

趣味推播 · ENGAGEMENT 😄

每日趣味推播 · Daily Engagement Content

Daily jokes, nostalgic topics, and trivia in Chinese — designed to spark joy, encourage daily app use, and keep seniors mentally engaged. Because connection is as important as safety.

對話偵測 · COGNITIVE CARE 🧠

對話模式偵測 · Conversation Pattern Detection

Our AI monitors subtle changes in conversation patterns over time. When meaningful shifts are detected, family members receive a gentle alert — not a diagnosis, but an early signal to seek professional guidance.

家屬後台 · FAMILY 👨‍👩‍👧

家屬管理後台 · Family Dashboard

Adult children manage everything from a dedicated dashboard — setting reminders, viewing activity summaries, managing emergency contacts, and staying connected to their parent's daily wellbeing from anywhere.

We believe that language should never be a barrier to safety, independence, or human connection. Our work is community-funded, community-tested, and driven by the conviction that the most vulnerable among us deserve the best of what technology can offer — not an afterthought.

關於我們 · About Us

我們是誰

PéiNín Foundation was founded by Chinese Americans who lived this problem firsthand. We watched our own family members — grandparents, parents, aunts and uncles — struggle with isolation, language barriers, and the quiet fear of being unreachable in an emergency. We knew the technology to help them existed. We also knew no one had built it for them.

We are technologists, caregivers, and community advocates. We have built an operational AI backend with Mandarin and Cantonese voice conversation, already tested with real seniors in the San Francisco Bay Area. We are not building this from a boardroom — we are building it from lived experience, user feedback, and deep respect for the community we serve.

我們的總部位於舊金山灣區，服務全美華裔社區。
Headquartered in the San Francisco Bay Area, proudly serving the greater Chinese American community across the United States.
